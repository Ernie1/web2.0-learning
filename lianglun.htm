Two theories is the concrete application of Marxist philosophy in China

Since the 18th CPC National Congress , Xi Jinping pointed out: "To learn philosophy and to use philosophy is a good tradition for our party." As a party armed with Marxism, it is the ruling party that grasps the ideology and grasps the ideological founding from the perspective of philosophy . Historical heritage.

During the period of China's democratic revolution, the entire party and the army still lacked a common and correct understanding method and ideological basis. Therefore, Comrade Mao Zedong used the basic ideas and methods of Marxist philosophy to profoundly sum up experiences and lessons from the Chinese revolutionary war, summed up philosophy, exposed the ideological roots of dogmatism and empiricism in the party, and established the pragmatic attitude of the party as a matter of fact , Wrote two philosophical essays, "Practical Theory" and "Contradictory Theory", used profound language to explain profound theories in simple terms, comprehensively and systematically explained the basic principles of Marxist materialist dialectics and epistemological methodology, and gave a brief account of how to use Marxist philosophy World outlook and methodology to deal with the various problems in the Chinese revolution and what is the solution to these two fundamental issues of dialectical materialism and historical materialism. The theory of practice and the theory of contradiction profoundly and thoroughly realized the creative application and development of Marxist philosophical thought, completely realized the sinicization of Marxist philosophy, and marked the fundamental formation of Marxist philosophy in China and Mao Zedong's philosophical thought.

As a epistemological book, "Practice Theory" emphasizes the necessity of combining Marxism-Leninism with the concrete practice of the Chinese revolution and emphasizes the weaknesses of the old materialism and the dogmatism ideological line.

First, it emphasizes the practical materialism of Marxist philosophy and emphasizes the scientific principle of the specific historical unification of understanding and practice. The basic status and decisive role of practice in understanding are fully and systematically expounded, which fully embodies the essence of practical materialism of Marxist philosophy. Second, using dialectic epistemology, we put forward the famous "two leaps" thesis that the active role of cognition not only lies in the positive leap from perceptual understanding to rational understanding, but also more importantly from the practice of rational understanding The leap of revolution fully embodies the dialectics of the process of cognition and further highlights the practical characteristics of Marxist philosophy. Thirdly, for the first time, we have summed up the general laws of human cognition for the first time and pointed out that "the practice, understanding, re-practice and re-cognition, the form, the reciprocity from endlessness to infinity, and the content of each cycle of practice and understanding Comparatively advanced to a higher level. " This is a new contribution in the history of Marxist philosophy.

As a dialectical work, according to the integration of Marxism-Leninism with the concrete practice of the Chinese revolution, "contradiction theory" seizes the key of metaphysics and the weaknesses of the dogmatic ideological method, and highlights its essence and core as an important part of materialist dialectics. Focus on the principle of the relationship between universality and particularity of contradictions.

First, the issue of the universality and the particularity that first proposes the contradiction is the essence of the problem of contradiction. If we do not understand it, it will be tantamount to abandoning the conclusion of dialectics . This is a new generalization in the history of Marxist philosophy. Second, it expresses that the space conflicts are everywhere and the time conflicts are endless, correct the metaphysical thinking that "differences are not contradictions", and thoroughly implement the universality of contradictions. 　Thirdly, it systematically exerts the special problems of contradictions, and discusses the particularities of contradictions in the forms of material movement. Fourth, creatively set forth the paradoxical imbalance theory.

The theme of "Theory of Practice" is the unity of understanding and practice. The theme of "Theory of Contradictions" is the unification of the universality and the particularity of contradictions. This "two unification" has laid a solid philosophical foundation for the ideological line of seeking truth from facts that combines the universal truth of Marxism-Leninism with the concrete practice of the Chinese revolution. These "two treatises" are the sharp ideological weapons opposing subjectivism in the Party, especially dogmatism.
